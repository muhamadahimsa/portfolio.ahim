BaC

The most comfortable cafe like your childhood home

BaC, where timeless charm meets the warmth of a neighborhood bakery and the comfort of a traditional coffeehouse. at BaC, we believe in the beauty of simplicity, and BaC is more than just a bakery, it's place where people gather to savor life's simple pleasures. When designing BaC I was referring to its characteristics, namely wanting to give the feel and feeling of a childhood home. The combination of colors, typography and also the vibe that is carried must be unified and related to each other, thus creating an identity that is in accordance with BaC.
