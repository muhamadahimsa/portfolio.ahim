Hi, I'm Muhamad Ahimsa, a designer and developer from Indonesia. I started studying in the design field as a graphic designer and in the web field as front-end developer, until finally switching to web design, UI/UX, and branding.

As a front-end developer I prefer developing websites with unique designs such as having animation and interaction, and trying to make them real, and that's what made me switch to website design armed with my previous design skills as a graphic designer and also front-end developer.

As a multidisciplinary digital designer, my services cover various areas of the design industry: UI animation, web design, mobile apps design, branding, social media design, art direction, identity, ecommerce, and print design.

My perspective on design is that I like designs that are simple and clean, but with a little touch of magic that makes it a pinpoint.

My workflow:

(001) Direction. A deep understanding of the brief and client is a must as a starting point. Determine project objectives and functional specifications, know the target audience, conduct research so as to find the right combination of Typography, Color, Composition, Movement and brand assets.

(002) Design. After collecting the right materials at the Direction stage, then I will explore and do research on competitors. I always create unique designs that emphasize the characteristics of each project, while being elegant and satisfying to use.

(003) Interaction and Animation. Motion and interaction are the things I like most when working on websites. These two elements create micro-interactions that enhance the user experience. That's the reason I switched to web design, because I really like this stage.

(004) Development. This stage is the most difficult but also fun. Creating smooth movements on a website so that it looks real is a fun job. Not only that, I ensure that the final product must match every element, color, typography, even device responsiveness.
